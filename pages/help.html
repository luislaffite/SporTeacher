How can we help you?

TEACHER

I want to offer my private lessons: How do I start?

To benefit from our student network (more than 60,000 daily student searches), we invite you to create a profile. If you have already created an account with us, you can post your first announcement. In order to make the most of your ad and get as many requests for lessons as possible, we advise you to take the time to write a full ad, to put a vertical image to illustrate your ad, to send us your diploma or certificate of studies and to ask for recommendations from your loved ones, colleagues, students, etc. According to our statistics, a complete profile with a photo is 100 times more likely to be requested. TRAINEE

How do I find a teacher?

It's very easy! The search for teachers is done from the search engine. Select the sport, enter your city, and that's it! Thousands of wonderful teachers, available and close to you or by webcam. Once you start the search you can refine your results: levels, price, distance... Then, when selecting the teacher or teachers, the student can contact them by clicking on the "Book a class" button, located under the teacher's profile photo and filling in the application form to get their "Student Pass", which is valid for 30 days. After sending the message to the teacher, he or she must accept the lesson request so that we can transmit your personal data to the student. üòÄ

How does Sporteacher always propose teachers who are available?

Sporteacher has established a system to measure the availability of teachers in real time and an analysis of the responses provided to other students. The teachers you see on the site are therefore available teachers. However, we invite you to specify your availability so that the teacher can verify that your request is compatible with their schedule.
